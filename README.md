## UX
### The main idea behind this webiste is to showcase a Gym's ideology of staying fit and healthy. The Gym's website plays a catalyst role for a user to live a healthy life style.  Anyone who loves staying fit or wants to change the daily boring routine will definitly be thinking of joining a Gym and a website provides a user with enough information and enthusiasm to join this Gym. The Gym specialize in many areas from Yoga to Zumba Dancing Classes. With Personnal training sessions and dietry plans encourage the users to carry on with the healthy life style. It also gives a way of meeting with the like minded people.
